Background on me

As a first-generation student at CU Denver, I've dedicated my academic and professional journey to bridging the gap between minorities and the privileged. My leadership roles, including serving as Co-President of my university's NSBE chapter, where I raised over $15,000 for regional and national conferences, have fueled my passion for creating opportunities for others. Our chapter was recognized as a 2023 Apple Black@Hardware scholarship winner, earning $5,000 and fostering a partnership with Apple's Black@Hardware group. In addition to my leadership experience, I bring technical expertise as a Code Instructor at CodeAdvantage and a SWE fellow at Headstarter AI, with proficiency in Python, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Java, and C++. I'm also the Co-Founder and leader of EmpowerYouthEverywhere, a nonprofit organization committed to elevating the next generation. I'm driven by a commitment to making a tangible impact in my community and beyond.
